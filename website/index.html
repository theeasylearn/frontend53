Our Story

About Us

Shree Hari Restaurant has been serving delicious food in the heart of Bhavnagar since 2000. For over two decades, we have been a favorite destination for families and food lovers who appreciate great taste, warm hospitality, and a welcoming ambiance.
From flavorful traditional dishes to popular multi-cuisine favorites, every meal is prepared with fresh ingredients and authentic recipes. Whether you're planning a family dinner, a casual outing with friends, or celebrating a special occasion, our comfortable atmosphere and exceptional taste make every visit a memorable experience.
